Through a series of miraculous events, the Lord God has blessed the Redeemed Christian Church of God with a sprawling campground on the outskirts of the Dallas area, USA

The first parcel of land acquired in the Year 2000 was 114 acres, but the total land space now (2024) is 850 acres. There are currently five buildings on the land:

  1. An administrative block that houses the headquarters office of RCCG North America, with a 1000-seater meeting hall.
  2. A 10000-seater auditorium at the heart of the 850-acre land.
  3. A 48-room hotel within walking distance of the auditorium or the office complex.
  4. A 70-seater chapel donated by a family in Nigeria and dedicated to the founder of the Church, Rev. Josiah Akindayomi.
  5. A pre-fabricated Youth Center, close to the Administrative Block and volleyball court.

Our shared dream, endorsed by many members and our esteemed General Overseer, is to position our campground as a versatile ‘destination spot.’ We envision hosting a wide array of events, from weddings and birthday celebrations to large-scale conventions and commencement programs for schools and colleges. We also aim to provide a serene setting for group and personal retreats and engaging youth camp events.

In June 2022, the General Overseer commissioned a fresh

“Camp Development” thrust to accomplish the above

The package proposed and approved would be:

  1. Beautification of the Pavilion perimeter, including the meditation park and lake.
  2. Creation of a Youth and Children’s Park to attract and keep young people.
  3. Creation of an RV/Cottage Park for versatile and affordable accommodation.

We invite everyone interested and able to donate to the funding of the various projects that will make the campground what we all dream it should be.
